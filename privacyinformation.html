Privacy Policy | Empower Estates Network LLC

Empower Estates Network LLC (“EEN,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) understands the importance of privacy to our customers, including personal information you provide and any sensitive data you may share in connection with our services. EEN is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining secure practices regarding any information you provide. This Privacy Policy applies to both the public portions of our website (open to members and visitors alike) and private portions (accessible after authentication), describing the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Coverage of EEN Website

This Privacy Policy applies only to our website (www.empowerestatesnetwork.com) and related services. When we refer to “we” or “EEN,” we mean Empower Estates Network LLC and any affiliated entities under our control. We may share information internally between affiliated entities, but all information remains protected under this Privacy Policy.

Please note that once you leave our website, other sites you visit may have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for how third-party websites treat your information.

Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. For minor updates that do not significantly affect the handling of your information, we will note changes at the end of the policy. For significant changes, we will obtain consent before using previously collected information in a new way and will post updates on our website promptly.

Information We Collect

From Non-Subscriber Visitors: Visitors may browse parts of our website without providing personally identifiable information. We may track technical information such as referring URL, browser type, access time, and date.

From Registered Users / Customers: Customers who register or enroll for services may provide identifying information, including name, address, and contact information. Registration forms clearly indicate required versus optional information. Users can update their personal information at any time through their online profile.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use session cookies to identify users during their visit. Cookies help personalize your experience, track website usage, and provide security protection. You may configure your browser to reject cookies; however, certain features may be unavailable without them.

Use of Information

Marketing and Advertising: We may use collected information to improve user experience or target marketing efforts. Advertisers or partners will not access personally identifiable information unless explicitly authorized.

Third-Party Sharing: We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Strategic Partners: We may share information with companies that provide co-branded services or marketing, but only in accordance with their privacy policies.
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us deliver services may have limited access to personal information but are contractually obligated to maintain privacy and security.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, the acquiring entity must comply with this Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to prevent harm.

EEN does not sell information collected via our website.

Security and Protection of Information

We maintain technology, policies, and procedures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. All employees and contractors are required to sign confidentiality agreements and comply with security measures.

User Responsibilities: Users must maintain the confidentiality of their account credentials and log off after sessions to prevent unauthorized access.

Security Practices and Technology:

  • Authentication: Users must log in using secure usernames and passwords.
  • Encryption: Sensitive information is encrypted using industry-standard SSL protocols.
  • Backup: Data is routinely backed up and stored in secure facilities.
  • Access Logs: All system access is logged to ensure accountability.
  • Firewall & Physical Security: Our servers are protected with firewalls and located in secure data centers with restricted access.

Access and Correction of Information

Users may update or correct personal information through their online profile. Requests for deletion may remove the user from our registry but certain anonymized data may be retained for statistical purposes.

Rights Regarding Data Collected

EEN is committed to compliance with applicable state and federal privacy laws, including HIPAA and CCPA. Users have the right to:

  • Know what information is collected.
  • Correct or delete information.
  • Non-discrimination and protection against retaliation.
